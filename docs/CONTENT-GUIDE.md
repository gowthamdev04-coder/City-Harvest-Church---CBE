# Content guide

These are the content items to collect before launch:

- Exact Sunday service times, address, maps link, parking notes and contact number.
- A welcome message from the lead pastor or leadership team.
- Details of children’s check-in, ages served and safety policy.
- Three to five genuine stories or testimonies, with permission to publish.
- Ministry leaders, meeting times, event registration details and sermon media links.
- Final giving provider link and legal/privacy information.
- A short, silent, landscape worship/service video for `client/public/media/hero-service.mp4`. Aim for 8–20 seconds, MP4/H.264, 1920×1080, and ideally under 8 MB for a fast homepage.

Temporary copy is deliberately marked as “Placeholder” in the homepage code so it is easy to find and replace.
